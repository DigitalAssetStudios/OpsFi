About OpsFi

Operations.Finance was founded in early 2022 as an offshoot of a leading Private Investment & Lending Fund Manager based in the UK and US. Our team is comprised of experts with over 25 years of combined experience, honed at top-tier firms like McKinsey & Company, Deloitte, and KPMG. Our mission centers around the power of Web3 to foster financial inclusion and improve quality of life worldwide. We support visionary founders to build financially sustainable enterprises in this space, working to establish Web3 as an indispensable part of the financial ecosystem. Drawing from our extensive expertise in high-growth SME development, investment fund management, and fintech, we are committed to enabling the growth and viability of Web3.

Empowering Financial Clarity:
Why We Do CFO Reporting

We understand that effective financial and strategic operations are essential for the sustainability and expansion of any enterprise. However, these functions can be costly, time-consuming, stressful, and prone to errors. Our CFO Reporting services seek to change this narrative by providing streamlined, premium solutions that deliver exceptional value for money. By leveraging our expertise, advanced technology infrastructure, and access to world-class talent, we are redefining CFO services for the Web3 era. Our comprehensive CFO Reporting solutions are designed to meet the unique needs of each client, ensuring accurate and actionable insights that empower decision-making and foster business growth. We’re here to simplify complex financial processes so founders can focus on their core mission.

Guiding Growth:
The Value of Our Advisory Services

At Operations.Finance, we take pride in collaborating with a diverse team of professionals across the USA, UK, and South Africa. Our Advisory services offer dynamic financial, operational, and strategic solutions tailored to support our clients’ growth and success. Together with our clients, we work toward a shared vision of shaping a future where Operations.Finance’s impact is recognized worldwide.
